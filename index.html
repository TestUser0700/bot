⚡ Live Mini-Player

A now playing interface that stays in sync — progress, queue changes, and loop toggles reflected automatically.

Jump, move, seek, or remove any track without typing the full title. Works across every command that needs it.

Seek by percentage, seconds, minutes, timestamp, or relative offset. The most flexible seek of any music bot.
