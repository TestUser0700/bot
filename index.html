Features
🎵 Live Player

A real-time now-playing embed with progress bar, album art, and full button-driven playback controls. No slash commands needed — just tap.

📋 Custom Playlists

Create, save, and manage your own private playlists. They're tied to your account and travel with you to every server that has MusikBot — your library, always at hand.

📊 Listening Profiles

A personal profile that travels with you. Earn ranks, see top artists, top tracks, listening streaks, peak hours, and more — all private by default, with full control over what others see.

🎛️ DJ Controls

Full playback control — skip, loop, shuffle, seek, volume, queue management, and DJ mode with configurable permissions.

🔍 Smart Search

Search across Spotify, Apple Music and SoundCloud with fuzzy autocomplete. Find and play music without leaving Discord.

⚡ Context Menus

Right-click integrations — view profiles, play Spotify status, play links, and browse playlists from context menus.

⏱️ Smart Seek

Instantly jump to any part of a track using intuitive time formats or intelligent autocomplete suggestions.

🪄 Fuzzy Autocomplete

Search through your queue, playlists, and history with lightning-fast fuzzy matching.
